The Map of the Unknown

This project is inspired by the idea that many of the deepest mysteries in science and mathematics are interconnected. By organising unanswered questions into a web of relationships, we hope to reveal hidden patterns and point towards deeper gaps in our understanding. For more background on the motivation for this project, see the accompanying reflection document.
